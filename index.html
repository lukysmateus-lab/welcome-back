Two Truths & A Lie

Let's see if you can trick your classmates! Write down 3 sentences about your vacation.

  • Two sentences must be TRUE.
    e.g., I went to the beach. I ate a whole pizza.
  • One sentence must be a LIE.
    e.g., I met Taylor Swift at the mall.
  • Read them aloud. The class will vote on which one is the lie!
